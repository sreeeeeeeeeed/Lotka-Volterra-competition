Interspecific Competition Model

Model: dN₁/dt = r₁N₁(1 - (N₁ + α₁₂N₂)/K₁), dN₂/dt = r₂N₂(1 - (N₂ + α₂₁N₁)/K₂). Here r is intrinsic growth, K is carrying capacity, and α terms are competition effects.

Population vs Time: trajectories of species 1 (N₁) and species 2 (N₂).

ZNGIs + Vector Field: the two sloped ZNGI lines are dN₁/dt = 0 and dN₂/dt = 0

Phase Space: system trajectory in (N₁, N₂) space.
